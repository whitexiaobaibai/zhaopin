举报收藏

职位描述：

• 参与大数据系统的架构研发，持续对系统的架构进行改进和优化，提升海量数据的查询性能和用户体验。

• 研发高性能业务处理技术，来满足快速大量的数据增长及复杂的业务逻辑，服务于数千家亚洲区客户； 

• 团队精神，具有产品思维，从产品角度定义、设计、开发出叫好又叫座的产品；

• 有性能优化和缓存部署经验； 

• 关注前沿技术发展趋势，学习最新后端框架。 

职位要求： 

• 计算机相关专业本科及以上学历，本科3年以上工作经验，硕士1年以上工作经验； 

• 优秀的分析问题、解决问题和快速学习能力，对解决具有挑战性的问题充满激情； 

• 对编码有激情，热衷开发简洁、高效、易维护的程序，符合行业标准； 

• 精通 JAVA开发,对 GC、多线程,同步机制有深入了解 , 熟练掌握 Eclipse 等开发工具； 

• 熟悉 J2EE主流框架及相关开发， spring、 hibernate、 struts2等； 

• 熟悉 HTTP通信协议 , 掌握 RESTFUL or SOAP Web 服务协议； 

• 熟悉和掌握至少一种源代码管理软件（ SVN/CVS/GIT）； 

• 熟练使用 Mysql/Oracle/MongoDB 等关系及非关系型数据库产品；

• 熟悉Lucene/ELK/Storm/Solr/Kafka/Redis 等框架优先；

• 善于沟通表达，具备良好的团队协作能力；性格乐观开朗，富于激情和创造力。

Wisers Information Limited.

  The technical foundation that underpins Wisers' service was originated in 1992 as an academic research project at the prestigious Chinese University of Hong Kong. This core technology led to the incorporation of Wisers Information Limited in 1998 and the launch of the first generation of WiseNews service, aggregating content from 20 sources in 1998.

  In 1999, international private equity firm, Walden International Investment Group provided funding from the Hong Kong SAR Government's Applied Research Fund for the further development of the Wisers' service. WiseNews Version 2.0 was launched in 2000. Wisers' development since then has been in response to its growing number of international clients, including many of the Fortune 500 firms.

  Today, Wisers now offers a suite of services and solutions targeted at corporations in various industries. The current iteration of WiseNews Version 3.0, brings updated daily information from over 3,000 sources across Greater China and Unitated States. Wisers shares 70% of the market in Hongkong, Taiwan and Macao. Now it has branches in Beijing, Shanghai, Najing and Shenzhen.
