举报收藏

岗位职责：

Partner with business team to lead vendors deliver projects on time in full. Projects including: data collection / integration among different platforms, data management, reporting ,data visualization and data analysis.

1.       Develop strategies for effective solution based on business requirement.

2.       Project management.

3.       Join UAT to ensure project fully delivered.

4.       Work out deployment manual.

5.       Change Request mgmt.

6.       Document archive (including solution wireframe, CR statement, quotation, data dictionary/flow, etc.)

7.       Troubleshoot data-related problems and authorize maintenance or modifications

8.       Assist with reports and data extraction when needed


任职要求：

Job Requirements:

1.       Proven experience as business analyst, 3~5 working experience is a must.

2.       Skillful in DMP data management and BI analytics, knowledge of Google Analytics, campaign performance analysis is prefer.

3.       Knowledge of Excel, SQL and R (or python); familiarity with business intelligence tools (e.g. Tableau, SAS)

4.       Outstanding analytical, problem solving, facilitation and presentation skills

5.       Graduated from top university in Statistics, Big data, Computer Science, Financial Engineering. Other backgrounds will also be considered.
