Introduction

For our CT in Beijing/Shanghai, we are looking for a Senior Software Engineer & Architect at earliest opportunity.

What are my responsibilities?
 



  • Work together with research / expert group for high-level analytics platform architecture & framework definition, application / solution design specification, etc.;

  • Design and improve the architectures of large-scale / distributed analytics platforms and applications;

  • Supervise implementation of key modules of platforms /applications;

  • Provide design & guidance of technical stack  for the development team;


What do I need to qualify for this job?
 



  • Master degree or above in Computer Science or equivalent;

  • 3+ years of software development experience, strong OO skills;

  • 2+ years of architecture design experience, incl. large-scale / distributed systems;

  • Development expertise in at least 2 of the listed:  Java, C#,  C++, JavaScript;

  • Familiar with the ecosystem of at least 2 of the languages above;

  • Experience on database and/or data warehouse platforms;

  • Self-motivated, good communication skills and good team player;

  • Fluent in English (speaking, reading and writing);
     

Preferred Qualifications
 



  • Experience on data analytics platform/application design and development;

  • Knowledge on open-source big data platform, like MapReduce, Storm, Hive, Spark, etc.;

  • Knowledge on typical data mining/machine learning algorithms, like regression, clustering, classification, recommendation, etc.;

  • Ability to handle multiple competing priorities in a fast-paced environment;


What else do I need to know?

CT
