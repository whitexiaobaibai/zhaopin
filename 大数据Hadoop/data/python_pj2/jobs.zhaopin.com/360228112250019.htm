Battaglia Advisory Services - Data Analyst
礡达企业管理咨询 – 数据分析师

Job Description 职位描述:

 * Design and execute full data analysis & statistical modelling starting from raw data under guidance of business strategy project management.
 在管理咨询项目中从原始数据中设计及执行数据分析及统计建模。

 * Develop data analytics within consulting projects to address consumer needs and opportunities.
 根据咨询项目的客户需求进行完整明确的数据开发。

 * Use and provide analysed data to assist customers in developing sales, marketing and customer insights.
 使用并提供分析后的数据协助客户在销售，市场及战略方面提供深度见解。

 * Coordinate with individuals or teams to complete analyses, task and projects on time with high quality.
 与个人及团队协作，以高质量完成数据分析报告。

Requirements 职位要求:

 * Master Degree is strong preferred from top university major in Data Mining, Statistics, Applied Mathematics. Bachelor degree also considered (with in depth experiences).
 硕士学历为首选，并毕业于国内外优秀大学专业为数据分析，统计学，信息科学，应用数学等背景。本科学历应具备丰富的项目经验。

 * Large consulting firm background or related sizeable consulting/Marketing research firm are required, with project experience of data analysing in different industry. Candidates from the likes of Accenture, Deloitte, Monitor etc. are welcome to apply.
 必须拥有大型或相关规模性咨询公司/市场调研公司工作背景，在多个（各行业）咨询项目中有丰富的数据分析经验。应聘者曾就职于埃森哲，德勤，摩立特等欢迎申请

 * At least 2~3 year of in depth experiences in Statistics and data mining, demonstrable skill in use of R or RStudio, as well as foundational experience in data visualization (Tableau). Comfortable in dealing with large datasets and designing and executing data mining tasks. Previous experience in Customer / Loyalty / Shopper insights strongly welcomed.
 至少一年以上深度运用R或RSTUDIO 进行数据统计、同样具备基础的数据可视化数据分析经验（Tableau）及处理大型数据集，熟练地运用大数据库并设计及执行数据挖掘等任务。有在顾客/忠诚度/购物者等以往项目经验中进行过数据分析的经验可视为核心加分项。

 * Practical and creative mind-set, able to “invent” and proactively make decisions. Not afraid of “blank page” situations.
 具有实用及创新的想法，能够运用自身独到的想法从而做出决定，无惧新项目挑战等。

 * Fluent English with Good communication skills.
 良好的中英语沟通能力
