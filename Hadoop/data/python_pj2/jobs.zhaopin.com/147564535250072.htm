举报收藏

岗位职责：

1、负责对数据库信息进行深度分析和有效利用；

2、建立及优化数据分析流程，提升分析效率；

3、负责业务数据整理及分析工作，提交数据分析报告；

4、大数据平台开发运维及优化；

5、研究人工智能技术解决城市发展问题、拓展城市设计应用。

任职要求：

1、有一定的数据统计和数据挖掘专业知识，具备利用数据分析解决业务问题的能力；

2、熟悉大型数据库,精通R语言、Python、数据挖掘；

4、具有较强的独立思考能力、创新能力和学习能力，擅长沟通；

5、具有较强的责任心，执行力及工作抗压能力，具备快速学习能力，愿意接受工作挑战；

6、信息工程、计算机工程、大数据分析等专业技术背景，专科以上学历；

7、有相关工作经验、有相关商务拓展能力的优先。

公司简介
德国ECS/易城国际总部设于德国杜塞尔多夫，分别在科隆、斯图加特、温哥华、上海等城市设有公司。ECS德国总部下含ECS与HW+P事务所，拥有110余名注册建筑师，他们分别来自德国、加拿大、瑞典等国，并在德国各州以及全球许多国家和地区从事规划、建筑、景观设计和专业性工程咨询顾问的服务。
自2004年进入中国后，ECS形成以德国总部技术支持，ECS（中国）独立运作的国际化管理模式和以上海为中心向全国幅射的市场化战略模式，公司设有规划部、建筑部、景观部、节能环保研究部、城市发展研究部、外事部等， ECS（中国）的国际化管理模式，赢取了最大程度理解本地项目需求和最先进的国际设计理念、技术创新信息的双重保障。公司一路稳健发展，至今公司项目已遍及上海、北京、天津、苏州、武汉、青岛、西安、等国内各大城市。在2009年中国境外设计事务所排名中，ECS（中国）名列第13位。
ECS的宗旨是以客户为本，以一流技术、职业精神顺应市场规律、客户的各种需要，为其提供全方位的服务。一直以来，我们以专业、高效的设计态度对待城市规划与建筑设计，以先进的设计内涵带给中国市场全新的规划、建筑、景观设计理念。在设计界树立起了一座亮丽的丰碑。
在长期的设计实践中，我们形成了八条共同思路来贯穿整个公司的工作。
一、追求卓越的精品意识
二、用理性思维进行艺术性工作
三、辨证地聆听业主的大计划
四、创新追求
五、集思广益
六、最优成本
七、完善后期服务
八、学术研讨
我们的设计理念是专业、理性、创新、诚信。我们将继续以精湛的设计、优秀的作品、高效的服务期待与各界的合作。
为了提供更优质的节能环保服务，ECS与西门子（中国）合作成立了易城西门子节能研究中心，旨为客户提供更好、更全面的整合性设计服务以及具有国际水准的专业建议。


ECS Engineering Consulting Service GmbH has its headquarters in Düsseldorf and branch offices in Cologne, Vancouver, Shanghai, etc. The Germany headquarter has over 110 professional registered architects and designers from German, Unite States, Canada, Sweden and etc. They are engaging in urban planning, architecture design and landscape design as well as professional engineering consultancy in many states of German and other countries all over the world.
After the company entered into Chinese market in 2004, ECS located its China headquarter in Bund Center Shanghai, to form independent international management mode technically supported by German headquarter and market strategy mode with the centre in shanghai and radiation to the whole country. Our company is consist of departments: planning, architecture design, landscape design, energy efficient research, urban development research and external affairs, which provide better and integrated services and professional consultancies with international standards to our clients. ECS China wins advanced international technique and optimized local condition under the international management mode.
Nowadays, ECS China has many projects covered in large cities such as Shanghai, Suzhou, Wuhan, Qingdao, Xian, Chongqing, Chengdu, Hefei and etc which help ECS wins good reputation in both design field and market. ECS (China) ranks 16 in overseas design offices in China in 2007.
The philosophy of ECS is client-oriented, and through high-class technology and professionalism to offer all-round services for meeting the markets and clients needs. ECS, featuring profound and advanced design ideas, upholding professional and efficient design and careful planning is bringing fresh and advance designs of urban planning, architecture and landscape to China and established a splendid model in the designing field.
In the long-term designing practice we concluded eight principles of guidance which penetrate the work of the whole company.
1. Pursue the highest quality standards.
2. Do artistic work which rational thought.
3. Listen to the direct plan of our client dialectically.
4. Work creatively.
5. Draw on the wisdom of the masses.
6. Optimize costs.
7. Improve later-stage services.
8. Engage in academic discussion.
Our faith in design is profession, ration, innovation and good faith. We will continue to provide exquisite design products and efficient services and look forward to cooperate with all walks of life。
ECS cooperates with Siemens and set ECS/Siemens energy conservation research center, aiming to offer better service for our clients.
