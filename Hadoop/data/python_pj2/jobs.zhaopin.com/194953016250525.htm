工作职责介绍

我们采用敏捷的、模块化开发方式，不同的Agile团队负责不同产品功能和模块的研发及测试。Automation Framework团队的职责是帮助每一个Agile团队搭建自动化的测试框架，根据不同团队（模块）的技术特性，选择不同的工具和方法。本团队会负责一些关键的自动化测试用例开发，而具体到每个模块的自动化测试用例，将由每个agile团队自己负责。此外，该团队还需与集成测试团队合作，帮助其搭建自动化的集成测试环境。作为Automation Framework团队的Lead，要领导该团队的人员、项目及技术，与各Agile团队及集成测试团队合作共赢，确保RSi自动化测试的高效和高质量。


工作地点

本职位工作地点在上海，但为保证与其他国家（美国、英国）同事的紧密沟通，候选人须能够接受短期出差。


教育背景 & 资质要求

- 计算机、数学、统计学、软件工程本科或研究生学历，4年以上行业工作经验；

- 可考虑经验不足但擅长快速学习的候选人；


技术 & 经验要求 

- 有较强的Java, python 开发经验 (5 年以上经验)；

- 熟悉OOP编程思想，有较强的接口设计能力和良好的编程习惯，而不只是使用过一些流行的Java framework来开发；

- 熟悉Junit, Ant/Maven, Jenkins等使用，了解Jenkins插件开发；

- 熟练掌握关系数据库以及SQL编程语言；

- 有商务智能（BI）、数据仓库类项目自动化测试经验；

- 如有OLAP、Vertica及其他大数据产品自动化测试经验则更佳；

- 有一定的项目及人员管理能力（领导过至少3人以上的项目）


个人特质

- 充满热情，有上进心；

- 极强的技术感知力，分析和解决问题的能力；

- 强烈的客户服务意识；

- 良好的团队合作精神；


About Engineering Department

Retail Solutions Inc. is the largest business intelligence service & solution provider in worldwide retailing market. As the Engineering Department, our mission is to design and implement the advanced global cloud computing platform, and deliver the valuable & actionable information to our customers, to help them promote their business.

Every day, we are extracting billions of data from 170+ retailers across the world(e.g. Wal-Mart, Metro, Carrefour and etc.), loading these data into our distributed data center, processing it and mining the valuable information out, and then delivering our services to the CPG companies through internet and mobile devices, our customer includes Unilever, P&G, Pepsi and etc.

We have 4 Main Engineering Teams located in different places:  Mountain View (Silicon Valley, US), London (UK), Pune (India) and Shanghai (China). Our China Team is the biggest one among the four.  We are looking for those candidates who have great passion to IT technology, good at cooperation and communications, and love to work in international team.  If you are the right guy, please join us!


JOB Description

In RSi, we are using agile model for product development.  We have several agile teams across different regions, and each agile team responsible for different component/Module.  The Automation framework team needs to work with each agile team, help them to set up the automation testing framework based on different team’s technology stack.  We also have an integration testing team that will integrate different component and run end to end testing, the Automation Framework team also needs to help them on the automatic integration testing framework.   As the lead of this team,  we need you to fully manage the people, project, technology of this team and work closely with other teams, to make sure RSi’s automation testing is in high quality and efficiency.


JOB Location

This position is based in Shanghai, but he/she need to do close communication with Colleagues from other countries (US, UK), so short term travels can’t be avoided.


Education & Certification Requirement

-Bachelor or Master Degree in CS/Mathematics/Statistics/Engineering with 4+ years working experience;

-Willing to consider less experienced candidates who are very bright and motivated to learn fast;


Knowledge & Experience Requirement

-Familiar with Java Programing Language;

-Good understanding on OOP methodology, capable of designing and implementing API;

-Familiar with Junit, Ant / Maven, Jenkins, understand how to implement Jenkins Plug-in;

-Good knowledge on SQL Language and Relational data base;

-Have experience of testing BI/DW related project/product;

-If candidate has OLAP or Vertica related experience, that will be a plug;

-Better to have project management experience with at least 3 team members.

 

Personal Attributes

-Passionate and highly self-motivated;

-Proactive, willing to own and drive things forward;

-Good written and oral communication skills (in both English and Mandarin), able to present ideas clearly through different channels: Emails, Meetings and Documentations;

-Strong Technical Sense and Proven Analytical / Problem Solving abilities;

-Ability to effectively prioritize and execute tasks in a high-pressure environment;

-Strong customer service orientation;

-Experience working in a team-oriented, collaborative environment.

瑞易RSi是全球零售行业中最大的商业智能和数据分析服务提供商，旨在为CPG（消费品）公司及零售商提供全球化的大数据解决方案。

瑞易服务于快速消费品和零售领域的500多家客户。每天，来自全球上百家大型零售商，超过12亿条数据进入瑞易的数据处理中心。瑞易帮助客户清洗、整合、标准化海量终端数据，并在此基础上提供智能数据分析平台和应用，继续深入挖掘数据价值，找到执行中的异常问题点，包括针对铺货、动销、库存、促销等客户关心的方面给出可执行的行动指南并且跟踪其落地。通过不断的创新与服务，瑞易已成功帮助多家全球和本土客户在中国市场取得成功，包括有：联合利华、金佰利、蒙牛、伊利、好丽友、百事、强生、南孚、可口可乐、立白、亿滋等。

瑞易一直是零供数据协同计划的重要推动力量，希望通过更广泛深入的数据分享将零供协作推向全新的高度。目前，瑞易在中国、北美、欧洲、澳大利亚、中南美与多个零售商及其供应链伙伴正在开展数据协同战略合作。

瑞易连续七年被评为快消品领域最佳客户服务公司，并在福布斯杂志中被评为零售行业“最大的SaaS公司”（SaaS：软件即服务）。瑞易总部位于美国硅谷，全球11个分公司。瑞易中国拥有百人以上的服务团队，我们充分了解本地零售商的商业流程和供应商的商业需求，旨在提供本土化的解决方案。
