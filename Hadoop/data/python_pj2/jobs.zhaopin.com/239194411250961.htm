北京福盛德经济咨询有限公司（Beijing Fost Economic Consulting Company Limited，FOST）是独立的第三方研究机构，专业从事中国宏观经济研究和中国经济政策分析，为国内外投资者、大型企业和政府部门等提供高品质的财经资讯和研究服务。

Beijing Fost Economic Consulting Company Limited (FOST) was founded in 2008 by Dr. Jianlin Feng. Dr. Feng has been deputy division director of the department of national economy of the National Development and Reform Commission of China (NDRC).FOST is known for its insightful analysis and accurate forecast of China’s economy and policies. Many financial institutes and large enterprises, such as ChinaAMC, Harvest Fund, Bank of Communications, ABC, Taikang Asset Management, China Minmetals Corporation, and SANY Group, are clients of FOST. PBoC and other government agencies also pay attention to FOST reports.
