任职要求：

1.       Bachelor degree or above, 5 years of major data architecture design experience; It is a priority for big Internet companies to be a big data architect本科以上学历，5年及以上大数据架构设计经验；在大型互联网企业从事大数据架构设计者优先考虑

3.       Has the profound theoretical basis of distributed system or database system, familiar with the working mechanisms of distributed computing systems, a distributed file system, distributed database system, such as cluster storage system architecture design experience具备深厚的分布式系统或数据库系统的理论基础，熟悉分布式计算系统的工作机制，具有分布式文件系统、分布式数据库系统、集群存储系统等架构设计经验；

4.       Have a deep understanding on big data infrastructure and platform, based on large data of Hadoop system have in-depth understanding, have the related products (Hadoop, Hive, HBase, Pig, Sqoop, Spark, etc.) project application research and development experience, with the development of the graphs of actual combat experience对大数据基础架构和平台有深刻理解，对基于Hadoop的大数据体系有深入认识，具备相关产品（Hadoop、Hive、HBase、Pig、Sqoop、Spark等）项目应用研发经验，有MapReduce开发的实战经验

5.       Familiar with common Internet business model and user behavior analysis model, familiar with the commonly used data mining techniques and algorithms, such as the path analysis, association rule mining, classification, clustering, classification, collaborative filtering, etc.熟悉互联网常见的业务模式和用户行为分析模型，熟悉常用的数据挖掘技术和算法，如路径分析、关联规则挖掘、分类、聚类分类、协同过滤等。

6.       Proficient in Linux/Unix environment and proficient in Java/c + + development; Bachelor degree or above, 5 years or above of major data architecture design experience; In large interconnection精通Linux/Unix环境，精通Java/C++开发； 本科以上学历，5年及以上大数据架构设计经验；在大型互联

7.       Have a passion for technology, can quickly accept and master new technology, has the strong independent, self learning ability, has the sense of responsibility, have strong problem solving skills, good communication ability and team spirit对技术有激情，能够快速接受和掌握新技术，有较强的独立、自助的学习能力，有责任心，具备较强解决问题的能力，有良好的沟通能力和团队精神。

8.       Good personality, good communication, careful thinking and clear logic.

性格开朗，善于沟通，思维缜密，逻辑清晰。
